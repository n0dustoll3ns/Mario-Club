Its a me, Mario

Mario is a video game series of platform games created by Nintendo. The series is one of the most popular in the world, and the best-selling video game franchise of all time. The first game in the series was released in 1985, and since then, over 200 games have been released. The games are primarily developed and published by Nintendo, although some other companies have developed or published them. The Mario franchise includes 18 main series titles, 30 spin-off games, and numerous ports to other systems.
